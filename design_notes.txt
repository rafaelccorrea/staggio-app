DRIBBBLE LOGIN DESIGN INSPIRATION:

Key trends observed:
1. Dark mode backgrounds with gradient overlays (deep navy/dark blue)
2. Large hero illustration or 3D element at top (40% of screen)
3. Clean white card/form area at bottom with rounded corners
4. Social login buttons (Google, Apple) with proper brand icons
5. Minimal text, clear hierarchy
6. Subtle animations on load
7. Glass morphism effects on some designs

DESIGN DIRECTION FOR STAGGIO LOGIN:
- Dark navy/deep blue gradient background (matching splash screen)
- Staggio logo PNG prominently displayed at top with glow effect
- Subtle animated particles or gradient orbs in background
- Clean white/frosted glass card for the form area
- Proper Google icon (not generic), Apple icon
- Smooth entrance animations
- "Bem-vindo ao Staggio" as hero text
- Tagline "IA para Corretores de Imóveis"

COLOR PALETTE:
- Background: #0A1628 → #0D2137 → #0F2B46 (dark gradient)
- Primary: #1E88E5 (blue)
- Accent: #00ACC1 → #26A69A (teal gradient, matching logo)
- Card: White with 0.95 opacity
- Text: White on dark, dark on card
